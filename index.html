Reborn Demons

Who Are We?

Essentially, we're the remainig members of Unstable Demons(and one close acquaintance of) coming back to reform the group. As we're from an official group, you can expect many fantastic artworks and blogs from us.

At this point, our goals are to become official once again and provide content for FTA. Our return date is sceduled at January 6th. Delays may occur however.

Notable Blogs


The Motivational and The Inspirational

A blog made by out second leader, Kini. Here she talks about relations between the characters of Fairy Tail and how we can learn from them. It underscores the importance of sentimental value in Fairy Tail.

Villanous Organizations: Raven Tail

The debut of the Villanous Organizations series written by Serena. This specific work talks about the ins and outs of the Raven Tail Guild. It's a very descriptive analysis with much more focus on perspective than others of it's kind.

Underrated Individuals

A blog made by our founder, Tadashi. He talks about many characters within the Fairy Tail series that deserved the spotlight. This blog set up the foundation for U.D's principles, focusing on topics that wouldn't be considered mainstresm, setting us out from others.
